\chapter{Foundational Quantum Algorithms}
\label{chap:quantum_algorithms}

Quantum computing paradigm offers fundamentally different approaches to solving computational problems compared to classical computing. 
This chapter explores two of the most significant quantum algorithms that demonstrate this quantum advantage: 
Grover's algorithm for unstructured search and Shor's algorithm for integer factorization. 

We will examine their theoretical foundations, mathematical proofs of correctness, 
and the implementation of these algorithms using the Qiskit framework.
